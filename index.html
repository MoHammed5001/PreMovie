Director: James Cameron
Release Year: 2009
Genre: Science Fiction, Action, Adventure
Rating: 7.8/10
Synopsis:

"Avatar" is set in the mid-22nd century on the fictional planet of Pandora. The story follows Jake Sully, a former Marine who becomes paralyzed and takes part in the Avatar Program. The program enables humans to remotely control genetically engineered bodies called avatars to interact with the indigenous humanoid species, the Na'vi. As Jake becomes more immersed in Pandora's world, he finds himself torn between loyalty to the human corporation exploiting Pandora's resources and his growing love for a Na'vi woman named Neytiri.
